Pizza

The best junk food!

We all know this, we need to eat something that goes fast, but we want that it tastes nice. This is the recipe to go.

The beauty with this recipe is that its very easy for everybody. So start cooking right know!

Ingredients

  • the first thing you need is something like ready-made dough
  • the second thing is a tomato sauce, I recommend a fresh one :)
  • Then, of course, the cheese. I always recommend to take fresh Gauda!

Steps

  1. First, roll out the dough with a rolling pin
  2. Then you spread a fine layer of tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. The last thing you do is sprinkle the cheese on the pizza and put it in the oven! Finished!
