Sandwitch

Very, very tasty.

This is how to make a easy sandwitch.

Do you know why sandwitches are so famous? Because there are so easy to make and you can tune them however you want, so lets go!

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Salami
  • And, of course, cheese

Steps

  1. Put the one slice of toast into your sandwitchmaker
  2. Now put the salami on the toast
  3. Finally put our cheese on top of the toast and turn the sandwitchmaker on!
  4. Note: You can tune your sandwitch however you want, this was just the basic sandwitch!
